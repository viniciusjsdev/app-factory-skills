# Example: App Launch Contract Excerpt

- Launch ID: `LAUNCH-20260713-01`
- Product: live beta of a household meal-planning app
- Objective: validate qualified signup-to-first-plan activation with Brazilian users
- Automatic execution: false

## Positioning

- Audience: adults responsible for weekday household meals who already improvise with notes, chat, or spreadsheets.
- Trigger: weekly planning causes repeated indecision and waste.
- Promise: organize a practical weekly plan in a few minutes using currently available product behavior.
- Prohibited claim: guaranteed savings or nutrition outcomes without evidence.
- CTA: create a free beta account and complete the first weekly plan.

## Instagram account setup kit

- Handle options: `planejacomida`, `meuplanosemanal`, `cardapiopraticoapp`; human must verify live availability.
- Display name: `Plano Semanal | Cardápio prático`
- Category: software/app, subject to current platform availability.
- Bio draft: `Planeje as refeições da semana sem planilha e sem improviso. Beta gratuito para testar seu primeiro plano.`
- Link: approved landing URL with `utm_source=instagram&utm_medium=organic&utm_campaign=beta_launch`.
- Human steps: create with organization-controlled contact, accept terms, set unique password, complete verification, enable 2FA, secure recovery, review roles, then authorize a minimum-permission connector.

## Creative experiment

- `EXP-001`: problem-led short video versus product-demo short video.
- Primary metric: activated first plans / unique landing visitors.
- Guardrails: complaint/block rate and misleading-claim review.
- External publish: requires final artifact approval and a separate commercial-operation contract.
