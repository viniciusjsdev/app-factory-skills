# Account setup kit

The kit tells the human owner what to create and what information to enter. It is not an account-creation automation.

## Required fields

- platform and purpose;
- legal/business owner and day-to-day operator;
- proposed handles in priority order and a live availability check step;
- display name and category;
- short/long bio within current platform limits;
- approved website/store URL and UTM convention;
- public contact method and privacy choice;
- profile image, cover, and icon brief;
- professional/business account settings;
- linked business/ads/analytics assets when in scope;
- content categories, highlights/pins, and first-post set;
- accessibility and language settings;
- support/moderation expectations.

## Human security checklist

- use an organization-controlled email/phone where appropriate;
- use a password manager and unique password;
- enable 2FA and store recovery codes securely;
- name at least two recovery/admin owners when the platform supports it;
- review connected apps, roles, billing, and recovery details;
- never commit or paste credentials, tokens, cookies, or recovery codes into the repository or agent prompt.

## Steps reserved for the human

Creating the account, accepting terms, choosing a live-available username, CAPTCHA, email/SMS verification, identity/business verification, password/recovery setup, 2FA, payment authorization, and final ownership review.

## Automation after setup

Only after the human completes ownership/security setup may a connector be authorized. Grant minimum permissions, record the connected account ID, test with a read-only operation, and require explicit confirmation for writes.
