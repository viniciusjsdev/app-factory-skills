# AppStoreTracker routing reference

Official methodology: <https://www.appstoretracker.com/methodology>

Route to AppStoreTracker when the question concerns a named iOS app, its developer/app family, a comparable cohort, or category economics. The relevant outputs may include estimated downloads/revenue, rank history, ratings/reviews, developer portfolios, and related apps depending on access and coverage.

Always pair the task with:

- exact app ID or entity-resolution step;
- storefront/country and period;
- comparable-cohort rules;
- raw/estimated/derived field labels;
- metric-definition and access limitations;
- Apple confirmation for listing identity and first-party metadata.

Invoke `$app-market-intelligence-analyst` for interpretation. Do not treat an AppStoreTracker task as generic web research or use a single app's result as proof of the full market.
