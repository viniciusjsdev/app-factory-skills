# Commercial approval policy

## Confirmation levels

- `none_required`: analysis, draft creation, setup guide, and other non-mutating work;
- `human_step`: account creation, authentication, connector authorization, verification, and 2FA;
- `explicit_action_confirmation`: public publish/schedule, direct contact, CRM mutation, deletion/correction, and any paid activity.

## Valid action confirmation

The confirmation must occur after the exact platform/account, content/artifact version, target or recipient set, schedule/time zone, URL, and cost are known. Record operation ID, approver, timestamp, and approved scope.

A general approval of the launch plan or creative pack is not approval to publish or contact.

## Reconfirmation

Require a new confirmation if content, target account, recipients, time, spend, distribution setting, or material platform preview changes. Authentication expiry alone does not broaden scope.

## High-risk defaults

- account creation remains human-controlled;
- mass outreach and synthetic engagement are prohibited;
- paid spend is blocked unless explicitly enabled with an exact cap and account;
- deletions, destructive edits, or corrections require an exact target and confirmation;
- secrets must never appear in contracts, prompts, receipts, or repository files.
