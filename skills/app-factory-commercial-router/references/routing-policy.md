# Commercial routing policy

## Codex owns

- strategy, positioning, offer framing, channel planning, and launch contracts;
- account setup information and human checklists;
- copy, scripts, creative briefs, assets, manifests, and quality review;
- operation preparation, metrics normalization, analysis, and next-experiment design.

## Human owns

- account creation and acceptance of terms;
- usernames at time of registration;
- passwords, recovery, CAPTCHA, email/SMS/identity verification, and 2FA;
- connector authorization and role/billing review;
- final confirmation for public writes, contact, mutations, and spend.

## Manus owns when configured and approved

- exact publish/schedule actions through a connected platform;
- bounded metrics retrieval;
- approved one-to-one contact with an explicit recipient list;
- approved CRM record updates;
- execution readback and receipt.

Manus does not own strategy, asset revision, recipient discovery, account creation, or autonomous engagement.

## Research boundary

Influencer, competitor, community, and live-platform discovery belong to the independent Research Factory and `$manus-platform-researcher`. The Commercial Router can reference an approved result but must not hide discovery inside an execution task.

## Fallback

When Manus is absent or a connector is not authorized, generate a human-execution package with final artifact, target, timing, caption/body, alt text, link, confirmation checklist, and expected receipt fields.
