# Commercial decision policy

## scale

Primary threshold passed, guardrails held, data is usable, and the result is repeatable enough for a bounded increase. Scaling still requires a new approved budget/action.

## iterate

The core audience/problem/offer remains plausible and one bounded message, creative, channel, page, or onboarding change can test the diagnosed bottleneck.

## pivot

Evidence contradicts a material audience, problem frame, channel, offer, or positioning choice, while a different framing has specific support worth testing.

## pause

Data is inconclusive/invalid, a dependency blocks a fair test, or the next test is not proportionate now. State the reopening condition.

## stop

Precommitted failure criteria were met with usable evidence, guardrails failed materially, economics/risk are untenable, or repeated bounded tests no longer justify more cost.

## Confidence

Base confidence on execution fidelity, metric quality, sample adequacy, consistency across sources, and sensitivity to assumptions. Never let high engagement inflate confidence when the decision metric is downstream activation or revenue.
