# Creative quality gate

## Strategy alignment

- The artifact targets the approved audience, message angle, channel, funnel stage, and CTA.
- The visible promise matches the product and launch contract.

## Claims and trust

- Every factual/performance claim is supported or removed.
- No invented testimonial, user count, award, urgency, scarcity, comparison, or guarantee.
- Regulated or sensitive claims have the required approval.

## Native execution

- The first frame/line communicates quickly.
- Copy is readable at the intended size and duration.
- The format respects confirmed current platform requirements.
- CTA and destination match.

## Accessibility

- Alt text or transcript exists where applicable.
- Captions/subtitles do not depend on audio alone.
- Contrast, text size, reading order, and flashing/motion risks are checked.

## Experiment integrity

- Variant IDs map to a hypothesis.
- The intended variable is identifiable.
- UTM/creative metadata is unique and consistent.

## Packaging

- Final files have deterministic names and versions.
- Source and export files are listed.
- Approval state is explicit.
- Nothing is labeled published or scheduled without an execution receipt.
