# Channel artifact specifications

Platform specifications change. Confirm current official limits before final production when exact dimensions, duration, text limits, or safe zones matter.

## Short video

Provide hook, duration target, scene/shot list, spoken script, on-screen text, proof moment, sound direction, caption, cover text, CTA, subtitles/transcript, and safe-zone notes.

## Carousel

Provide objective, slide count, one idea per slide, cover hook, progression, proof, final CTA, caption, alt text, and visual system. Avoid unreadable paragraph slides.

## Stories

Provide frame sequence, visible text, interaction element where approved, link destination, CTA, duration/asset note, and accessibility text.

## Static post/ad

Provide placement, aspect/dimensions after official confirmation, headline, supporting text, visual hierarchy, CTA, caption/body variants, proof, and alt text.

## Outreach draft

Provide recipient segment, personalization fields, subject/opening when relevant, concise relevance, honest ask, compensation/terms placeholder if applicable, opt-out/respectful close, and follow-up limit. Output is a draft, not approval to send.

## Landing/store copy

Align headline, proof, CTA, objections, screenshots, and conversion event with the creative. Do not promise functionality not present in the approved product truth.
