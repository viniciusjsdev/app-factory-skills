# Marketing Factory Workflow

1. Confirm that commercial launch/marketing work was explicitly requested; do not make it a prerequisite for MVP construction.
2. Use `$commercial-launch-architect` to define audience, positioning, offer, channels, account setup kits, phases, KPIs, attribution, and experiments.
3. Give the human owner the account-creation/security checklist. Never collect or store credentials, verification codes, recovery codes, or 2FA secrets.
4. Use `$marketing-creative-builder` to produce approved channel-ready assets, copy, variants, accessibility fields, UTM metadata, and a manifest.
5. Use `$app-factory-commercial-router` for each bounded next operation.
6. Keep strategy and artifact work in Codex; route account/security/confirmation/spend to the human.
7. Send only exact approved native actions or metrics reads to `$manus-commercial-operator`.
8. Require and validate an execution receipt. Do not infer success from submission.
9. Use `$commercial-validation-analyst` to reconcile execution, funnel metrics, cost, and feedback.
10. Return scale/iterate/pivot/pause/stop and an optional next experiment without triggering it.
