# Research Validation Checklist

## Invocation

- [ ] Research was explicitly requested.
- [ ] MVP and Marketing workflows remain independent/inactive unless separately requested.

## Contract

- [ ] Decision, owner, deadline, market frame, and depth are explicit.
- [ ] Critical hypotheses have support thresholds and falsifiers.
- [ ] Geography, platform, period, freshness, inclusion/exclusion, and budgets are bounded.

## Evidence

- [ ] Original source and retrieval mechanism are separate.
- [ ] AppStoreTracker/other modeled values are labeled estimates.
- [ ] Apple/AppStoreTracker or repeated reports are not double-counted as independent evidence.
- [ ] OpenAlex discovery points to original works.
- [ ] Platform-native research is read-only and contains no private/secret data.
- [ ] Search failures, missingness, contradictions, and transformations are preserved.

## Decision

- [ ] Devil and angel cases use the same evidence standard.
- [ ] Methodology, sample, periods, and calculations were audited.
- [ ] Outcome and confidence match the weakest critical evidence.
- [ ] Next experiment has pass/fail, guardrails, cost/time cap, and stop rule.
- [ ] `automatic_handoff` is false.
