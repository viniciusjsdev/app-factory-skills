# Marketing Launch Checklist

## Invocation and truth

- [ ] Marketing was explicitly requested.
- [ ] Product claims are proven, hypotheses, or prohibited.
- [ ] Audience, offer, channel, CTA, destination, and conversion event are approved.

## Accounts

- [ ] Owner/operator and recovery responsibility are clear.
- [ ] Codex produced setup fields and visual/content briefs.
- [ ] Human creates the account, controls credentials, verification, 2FA, recovery, connectors, roles, and billing.
- [ ] No secret appears in repository artifacts or prompts.

## Creative

- [ ] Format requirements were checked against current official platform guidance.
- [ ] Claims, CTA, accessibility, variants, files, and UTM metadata passed QA.
- [ ] Artifact/version and approval state are immutable for the operation.

## Execution

- [ ] Discovery/research is separate from the write task.
- [ ] Target account, content, recipients, schedule/time zone, link, limits, and cost are exact.
- [ ] Public writes/contact/CRM/spend received post-preview confirmation.
- [ ] Manus task is bounded and contains no strategy, rewriting, or recipient discovery.
- [ ] Receipt verifies native IDs/URLs/readback; ambiguous writes were not retried blindly.

## Validation

- [ ] Planned and executed states were reconciled.
- [ ] Metrics have definitions, denominators, attribution window, cost, and quality status.
- [ ] Outcome is scale/iterate/pivot/pause/stop with a measurable next step.
- [ ] No next external action or cross-factory handoff started automatically.
