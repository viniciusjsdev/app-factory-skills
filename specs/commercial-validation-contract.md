# Commercial Validation Contract

## Input

Commercial launch/experiment contract, creative manifest, execution receipts, platform/product/CRM metrics, costs, feedback, and tracking definitions.

## Required analysis

- planned-versus-executed reconciliation;
- data-quality status and exclusions;
- funnel counts, denominators, rates, costs, attribution, and uncertainty;
- threshold/guardrail comparison and pass/fail/inconclusive/invalid classification;
- observed facts separated from inference/assumption;
- diagnosed bottleneck and smallest next experiment.

## Outcome

Choose `scale`, `iterate`, `pivot`, `pause`, or `stop` with confidence and rationale. Any new external action or cross-factory handoff remains inactive until explicitly approved.
