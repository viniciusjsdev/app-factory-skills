# Platform Research Contract

## Input

Task/decision ID, platform/session authority, query, audience, geography/language/time, inclusion/exclusion, maximum result count, required fields, privacy constraints, and completion condition.

## Allowed output

Creator, community, competitor, or content-pattern records with stable handle/URL, observable metrics and timestamp, relevance evidence, recent matching examples, risks, confidence, limitations, and search failures.

## Boundary

The task is read-only. It cannot create accounts, contact, follow, like, comment, publish, schedule, purchase, modify settings, or expose credentials/private data. Every result states `external_state_changed: false`.
