# Research Provider Routing Contract

## Responsibility

Route bounded tasks according to evidence need, authentication, availability, cost, and source quality.

## Routes

- focused open web and synthesis: Codex;
- iOS app/developer/family/cohort intelligence: `app-market-intelligence-analyst` with AppStoreTracker plus Apple;
- scholarly discovery: OpenAlex, followed by original works;
- broad open-web discovery: optional Perplexity with Codex source audit;
- native/authenticated platform discovery: `manus-platform-researcher`;
- missing paid/connected provider: free or human-assisted fallback with a recorded coverage gap.

## Required route record

Research/task IDs, evidence class, primary/fallback route, reason, readiness, exact filters/query, limits, provider permissions, warnings, and expected evidence schema.

Routing never counts providers as independent evidence, starts another factory, or authorizes platform writes.
