# Commercial Operation Contract

## Purpose

Authorize and bound one external commercial action after final content and target are known.

## Required fields

- `operation_id`, version, mode, platform, connected account ID;
- immutable artifact/content reference and approved preview;
- exact recipients/objects, schedule/time zone, link/UTM, accessibility fields;
- confirmation status, approver, timestamp, and scope;
- item/retry/time/spend limits;
- expected verification evidence and correction path;
- prohibited data and actions.

Supported Manus modes are publish, schedule, approved contact, metrics read, and CRM update. Account creation, credentials/verification/2FA, strategy, discovery, recipient expansion, mass engagement, and unapproved spend are prohibited.
