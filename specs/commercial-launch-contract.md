# Commercial Launch Contract

## Purpose

Define an executable commercial launch for any product without assuming prior Research or MVP work.

## Required fields

- launch/version/status, owners, geography/language, timeline, and budget/cost cap;
- commercial decision and objective;
- product truth/proof and claim status;
- audience, buyer, trigger, desired outcome, alternative, positioning, objections, and proof;
- offer, pricing/test condition, CTA, destination, conversion and activation events;
- selected/excluded channels and rationale;
- account setup kit for each channel;
- launch phases, tasks, owners, dependencies, approvals, and stop conditions;
- creative requirements;
- KPI, funnel, attribution/UTM, reporting, and guardrails;
- experiment hypotheses, thresholds, and cost/time caps;
- risk/open questions and optional handoffs.

Account creation/security actions and public execution are outside this contract.
