# Market Validation Contract

## Input

Research contract, normalized evidence bundle, calculations, limitations, counterevidence, and search-failure log.

## Review

Apply research lead, methodology/data-quality, devil, angel, arbiter, evidence auditor, experiment designer, and scribe lenses. Label material statements as Evidence, Inference, Assumption, Open question, or Search failure.

## Outcome

Choose exactly one: `proceed_to_product_brief`, `experiment_first`, `revise_search`, `revise_hypothesis`, `pause`, or `reject`. Include confidence, critical evidence IDs, rationale, sensitivity, and the smallest useful next experiment.

## Boundary

The outcome may name an optional handoff but must set `automatic_handoff: false`.
